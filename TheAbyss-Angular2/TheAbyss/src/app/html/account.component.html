      Where is The Abyss based?

      The Abyss LTD incorporated in Malta, registration No.С 85718, registered office at: Orange Point Building, Second Floor, Dun Karm Street, Birkirkara By-Pass, Birkirkara BKR 9037, Malta

      What is The Abyss?

      The Abyss is a next-generation digital distribution platform, delivering all types of video games (Free2play MMOs and cryptogames being a key priority), including AAA-titles, to the fast-growing global game community.

      What is the difference between The Abyss and other game platforms?

      Unlike other platforms (Steam, Origin, GOG, etc.), The Abyss offers a groundbreaking motivational and multilevel referral system, allowing gamers to earn from in-game and social activities, and other gamers’ payments as well. By joining The Abyss, developers will reduce their marketing expenses and receive an extra income from referral payments made in other games on the platform.

      What is the purpose of applying the blockchain technology to the game platform?

      The blockchain technology is very promising for the game industry, since it allows to enjoy all the advantages of cryptocurrencies and tokens. ABYSS tokens (ERC20 standard on the Ethereum blockchain) are a priority internal mechanism for interaction on The Abyss platform. Earnings from all referral and motivational programs are paid in ABYSS tokens. Besides, the majority of internal services will rely on ABYSS tokens only.

      What is the Token Sale?

      The Token Sale is a process of selling ABYSS tokens created by the project team in order to raise funds for (among other issues) the project launch and development.

      What is DAICO?

      DAICO is an innovative fundraising model ( suggested by the founder of Ethereum, Vitalik Buterin) that merges some of the benefits of Decentralized Autonomous Organizations (DAOs), aimed at upgrading and making the initial ICO concept more transparent and secure. The Abyss Token Sale is an advanced and improved DAICO model, allowing contributors to control the withdrawal of funds, and also providing an option to vote for refund of the contributed money in case the team fails to implement the project.

      What is the Token Sale (DAICO) timeline? Will it imply any bonuses?

      DAICO starts on April 18, 2018. Participants will be granted 25% to 5% bonus for the amount of tokens they have purchased during the Token Sale period.

      What is the Token Sale (DAICO) web address?

      The Token Sale (DAICO) web address is https://www.theabyss.com

      How can I purchase the ABYSS tokens? What payment methods will be acceptable?

      We will accept Ethereum (ETH) and Binance (BNB) tokens payment methods.

      Please, note that BNB tokens are accepted from the international (non-U.S.) participants only. The minimum contribution for Binance tokens is 1000 BNB, and there’s no maximum contribution. The Binance tokens Hard Cap is 300.000 BNB. Since sending BNB tokens to the Smart Contract is a complicated procedure, we recommend it to the experienced users only.

      Can I use fiat money for the ABYSS tokens purchase?

      No. We will accept Ethereum (ETH) and Binance (BNB) tokens payment methods. You can use fiat money to buy ETH on Bittrex, Poloniex, Kraken, Bitfinex, Bitstamp or any other dedicated portal.

      Should I pass the KYC / AML procedure to participate in Token Sale (DAICO)?

      Yes. We will run a KYC / AML program for everyone. In addition, the U.S. citizens should pass the accreditation process. The respective instructions on passing the KYC / AML process will follow.

      Will you enable a Whitelist?

      Yes. Everyone who registers and passes the KYC / AML procedure successfully, will be whitelisted and have a guaranteed right to participate. However, we reserve the right to stop whitelisting any time.

      What are the minimum and maximum contributions during the Token Sale (DAICO)?

      The minimum contribution for first 24HRS is 0.2 ETH, maximum - 20 ETH. Starting from the 2nd day the minimum contribution remains the same, with no cap limit.

      The minimum contribution for Binance tokens is 1000 BNB, and there’s no cap limit.

      Please, note that BNB tokens are accepted from the international (non U.S.) participants only.

      What is the Soft Cap and Hard Cap in regard to the Token Sale (DAICO)?

      The Soft Cap is a minimum level of funds raised, that allows to consider the Token Sale successful. The Abyss project Soft Cap is 11 538 ETH. If we fail to reach this level, all funds will be returned by the Smart Contract.

      Hard Cap is a maximum amount of funds raised. The Hard Cap is 34 615 ETH, and the additional Hard Cap is 300 000 BNB.

      What can I use the ABYSS tokens for?

      You can use the ABYSS tokens for the purchases on the platform. Unlike payments, made with fiat money, using ABYSS tokens will get you a discount at the expense of the platform.

      Besides, we plan to introduce the additional opportunities for all token holders. In particular, Referral and Motivational programs, Auction, Syndicates and Internal CPA Network will rely on ABYSS tokens only.

      ABYSS tokens can be freely transferred to friends within the framework of the platform or withdrawn to cryptocurrency account (both manually and automatically). With the development of the platform more options for ABYSS tokens users will be added.

      Who is restricted from participating in the Token Sale?

      Citizens and residents of Algeria, Morocco, Ecuador, Kyrgyzstan, Bolivia, Bangladesh and Mainland China are restricted and will not be allowed to participate in our Token Sale (DAICO).
