crypto reward ecosystem for gamers & developers

The Abyss is a next-generation digital distribution platform, delivering all types of video games (Free2play MMOs and cryptogames being a key priority), including AAA-titles, to the fast-growing global game community. Unlike other platforms (Steam, Origin, GOG, etc.), The Abyss offers a groundbreaking motivational and multilevel referral system, allowing gamers to earn from in-game and social activities, and other gamers’ payments as well. By joining The Abyss, developers will reduce their marketing expenses and receive an extra income from referral payments made in other games on the platform.

GAMERS' BENEFITS

  • Personal five-level referral program
  • Earnings from personal and joint achievements
  • Monetization of content created
  • Earning extra income at in-game auction
  • Syndication to earn from joint activities
  • Tokens withdrawn to personal Ethereum-wallet

DEVELOPERS' BENEFITS

  • Personal five-level referral program
  • Target and content-generating audience at the start of the project
  • Access to specialized game statistics
  • Multilingual 24/7 customer support service
  • Convenient traffic rotation mechanism in every game on the platform
  • Simple techniques to get internal and external traffic

ABYSS Tokens: The Key Benefits

ABYSS tokens are a priority internal mechanism for interaction on The Abyss platform. Earnings from referral and motivational programs are paid in ABYSS tokens. The majority of internal services rely on ABYSS tokens only

ACCESS


Motivational and Referral programs, Auction, Internal CPA Network and Syndicates rely on ABYSS tokens only

Discounts


Unlike fiat payments, when paying for game purchases with ABYSS tokens, users are granted a discount

BURNING


1/3 of the platform’s commission from the internal CPA network and the auction transactions will be burnt by the Smart Contract

Browser online games on the platform

Client online games on the platform
Games where all users have an ability to affect other users' gameplay.
Early stage access to games for testing and bugs fixing with users' help
The Abyss platform allows to run the games in test mode, i.e. the game still requires improvements and bugs fixing. All this can be done with the gamers' help.
Cryptogames allowing for rewards and payments in cryptocurrencies
We stand at the very beginning of the emergence of games that take into account modern trends, allowing you to spend, exchange and receive cryptocurrency. What are the cryptogames, so far no one can tell for sure. But there's no doubt that they will come into sight.
Social Features
Game reviews, allowing to get an insight into the game
Game developers can set specific tasks for gamers, rewarding them with tokens. One of these tasks is writing a review - a detailed story covering all aspects of the game, and helping other users to decide whether to play this game or not.
User comments, allowing to accumulate maximum opinions about the game
Comments help to share users' opinions, assign ratings to games and navigate other gamers.
Game communities for exchanging opinions and game content
Sometimes forum is not enough to satisfy the users' need for communication. With the permission of the developers, the dedicated game communities can be built, allowing for the creation of fan art, mods, localization and other types of creatives.
News feed showing friends' in-game progress and other activities on the platform
What games do your friends play? What achievements have they received? Which activities are they engaged in? All this is available in a fully customizable news feed.
Ability to present games to friends using your personal account
You can make a present to your friend or a relative. Just buy the game and choose someone to make a present.
Unified forum for platform's users
The forum provides a ground for communication between all platform's users, and helps to build gamers' communities. The forum covers various projects and also has specific topics threads where you can share your opinion on various issues, find new friends, and ask questions you're interested.
The average game rating assigned by Metacritic and Gamespot
If the game is featured on Metacritic and/or Gamespot, you will see its average rating in the description and will have an ability to reach both sites by a single click. This will give you a full impression of the project.
Auction of in-game items (buy/sale/exchange) for various game users
The auction allows to sell in-game items (which you have received playing games) to other users, as well as buy items off them. The auction is not tied to games - you can expose any item that the developer allows to put up for sale.
Ability to unite in syndicates to gain additional earnings
Syndicate is a united game activity allowing to gain additional earnings depending on its rating. Gamers can join the syndicates despite of the concrete game.
Motivational Programs
Various achievements for investigating the platform's features
Achievements are different goals that can be set and achieved in the process of investigating the platform's functionality. Achievements reflect the user's involvement in the game environment.
Reward payments for receiving achievements
In-game achievements are rewarded with ABYSS tokens. The higher the rarity level of the achievement, the higher the reward is.
5-level referral program for gamers and developers
The referral program, allowing gamers to build their own referral networks comprising the invited friends and friends of their friends. Gamers receive a share of referral payments on their personal accounts.
Developers can also build referral networks comprising new gamers they have invited. The developer receives a share of referral payments madein other games on the platform.
Internal CPA Network for developers
The system for managing and exchanging traffic for the developers. Sell useless traffic to other games that can make use of it. By doing this, you can compensate the expenses for its purchase.
Motivation system for gamers, stimulating to invite more friends on the platform
The opportunity of receiving a share of your friends' and their friends' payments is a strong motivation.
Motivation system for developers, stimulating to invite more users on the platform
The opportunity of tying new users to your game and receiving a share of your gamers' payments (and their referrals' as well) in other games is a strong motivation.
More Features
"Out of network" mode, allowing to get access to platform's various features even if there's no internet connection
When disconnected, many of the platform's features are unavailable. The Abyss' client will leave only those features available, that do not require an Internet connection.
Ability to take screenshots of game process for storing on the PC and sharing with friends
Take screenshots and share the most memorable and exciting game moments with your friends.
Ability to capture videos of game process for storing on PC and sharing with friends
Capture the video game process to share with friends.
Virtual local network for games with no developers' support
Building a VLAN allows to unite distant computers into a virtual local area network (even if these computers are located in different cities/countries/continents). With virtual local area network you can play games that have no official servers available.
Client support service integrated into games on the platform
The developer can integrate the platform's support service into his game, providing a high-quality support to its users.
Cryptocurrency wallet tied to your personal account on the platform
You can tie your crypto wallet to a personal account on the platform to simplify the procedure of withdrawal or purchase of ABYSS tokens, or exchange them for another currency.
Token as a basic currency unit on the platform
Cryptocurrency that can be obtained on the platform through referral payouts or rewards. Tokens can be withdrawn from the platform or freely transferred to other users.
Crowdfunding
Crowdfunding platform, allowing to raise funds for new exciting projects.
